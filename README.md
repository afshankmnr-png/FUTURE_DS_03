# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze the Bank Marketing Campaign dataset to evaluate marketing campaign performance, customer conversions, and identify factors that improve conversion rates.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Google Colab
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Dataset
Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset (bank.csv)

## Analysis Performed
- Data loading and preprocessing
- Data cleaning and duplicate removal
- Total customers analysis
- Campaign conversion analysis
- Conversion rate calculation
- Contact method analysis
- Monthly campaign analysis
- Data visualization using bar charts

## Key Insights
- The marketing campaign contacted a large number of customers.
- The overall conversion rate was calculated to measure campaign effectiveness.
- Most customers did not subscribe to the campaign, while a smaller percentage converted successfully.
- Contact methods influenced customer responses.
- Campaign performance varied across different months.
- Focusing on effective contact methods and campaign timing can improve future conversion rates.

## Conclusion
The analysis shows that marketing campaigns can be optimized by targeting the right customers, selecting effective communication channels, and running campaigns during high-performing periods. These improvements can increase customer conversions and overall marketing performance.
